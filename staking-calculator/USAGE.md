# Usage Guide

The Cardano Staking Calculator is available on the official Blockiy website.

## How to Use

1. Open the tool:
   https://www.blockiy.com/cardano-staking-calculator/

2. Enter your ADA amount and any available parameters.

3. Review the estimated staking rewards generated by the calculator.

## Notes

The displayed values are estimates and should be used for informational purposes only.

Actual staking rewards may vary depending on network conditions, pool performance, fees, and protocol parameters.
