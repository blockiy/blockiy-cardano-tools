# Usage Guide

The Cardano Fear & Greed Index is available on the official Blockiy website.

## How to Use

1. Open the tool:
   https://www.blockiy.com/cardano-fear-and-greed-index/

2. View the current Fear & Greed score.

3. Explore the market sentiment indicator as part of your own research and decision-making process.

## Notes

The Fear & Greed Index is provided for informational and educational purposes only.

It should not be considered financial or investment advice.
