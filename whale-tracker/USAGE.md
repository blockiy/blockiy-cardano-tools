# Usage Guide

The Cardano Whale Tracker is available on the official Blockiy website.

## How to Use

1. Open the tool:
   https://www.blockiy.com/whale-tracker/

2. Review the latest whale activity and transaction summaries.

3. Use the information as part of your own market research and analysis.

## Notes

The Whale Tracker is provided for informational and educational purposes only.

Users should always conduct their own research before making financial or investment decisions.
